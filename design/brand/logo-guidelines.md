# Logo & Icon Guidelines

## The Learnmap mark

The Learnmap wordmark consists of two parts:
1. **Icon** — a stylised lightning-bolt / map-pin glyph in Brand Violet
2. **Wordmark** — "Learnmap" set in Geist Variable, weight 700

---

## Logo lockups

### Primary lockup (horizontal)
Icon + wordmark side-by-side. Use this as the default in navigation bars and headers.

### Icon-only
Used for favicons, app icons (≤32 px), social avatars, and loading states.

### Wordmark-only
Acceptable when the icon has already appeared on the same page or screen.

---

## Clear space

Maintain a minimum clear space equal to the **cap-height of the "L" in Learnmap** on all sides of the logo lockup.

Do not place any text, image, or graphic element within this clear space.

---

## Minimum sizes

| Lockup | Minimum width |
|--------|--------------|
| Horizontal (icon + wordmark) | 120 px |
| Icon-only | 20 px |
| Wordmark-only | 100 px |

---

## Colour usage

| Background | Logo treatment |
|------------|----------------|
| White / light surfaces | Icon in `brand-violet-600` (#7C3AED), wordmark in `neutral-900` (#171717) |
| Dark surfaces | Icon in `brand-violet-400` (#A78BFA), wordmark in `neutral-50` (#FAFAFA) |
| Brand Violet surface | Full white version (icon + wordmark both `#FFFFFF`) |

---

## What NOT to do

- Do not recolour the icon to any colour outside the approved palette.
- Do not stretch, rotate, or skew the logo.
- Do not apply drop shadows, outlines, or effects.
- Do not place the primary lockup on a busy photographic background without an adequate scrim.
- Do not recreate the wordmark in another typeface.
- Do not use the logo at sizes smaller than the minimums above.

---

## File formats

| Format | Use case |
|--------|----------|
| `.svg` | Web, all scalable contexts |
| `.png` (2×, 3×) | App icons, OG images, email |
| `.ico` | Favicon only |
